## User stories
### User story of Luna
As a ecofriendly, non-waste believer, I want to have the opportunity to buy an ecological soap where I can pick my own ingredients and scents so that the soap can match my hair or body-type and I can decide of which ingredients the soap is built.

### User story of Ruth
As a mother of two kids who's environmentally conscious, I want to make my own ecological soap without losing time that I could spent with my kids. So I need a online store where I can customize my own soap online, and they can produce it for me.
